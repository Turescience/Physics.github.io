Paper Mills and Research Integrity in Cosmology: Investigating P-V Phase Transitions in Massive Gravitational Systems

Abstract: We will use a discussion of some recently encountered papers to explain the phenomenon of "paper mills" in the context of cosmological thermodynamics. We explored how these operations produce papers, discussed their harmful effects, and examined the internal and external factors contributing to this issue. Additionally, we expressed our hopes for the healthy development of research in this field.
